Dispensaries on Social Media

Research Question:

How do dispensaries use Twitter to engage with medical cannabis patients and their larger follower base?


Hypothesis:

Communities of users form around dispensaries dependent upon how the dispensaries themselves use Twitter.

Analytic Plan

  1. Acquire Follower IDs for Selected Dispensaries Twitter Accounts
  2. Using followers shared between two dispensaries, find communities of dispensaries with network analysis approach
  3. Develop metrics based on dispensaries cyberbehaviors on Twitter
  4. Use metrics in a predictive model to predict community membership
